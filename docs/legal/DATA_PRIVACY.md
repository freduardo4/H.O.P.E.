# Data Privacy & Retention Policy

H.O.P.E. is committed to protecting user data and vehicle telemetry.

## Data Collected
- **Vehicle Telemetry**: RPM, Speed, MAF, MAP, Fuel Trims (Time-series data).
- **User Info**: Username, email (for account management).
- **Diagnostics**: DTC codes, VIN (hashed in non-admin views).

## Data Retention
- **Telemetry**: Stored for 30 days in hot storage, archived to cold storage for 1 year, then deleted.
- **Account Data**: Retained until account deletion request.

## GDPR / CCPA Compliance
- **Right to Access**: Users can export all their data via the "Download My Data" button in settings.
- **Right to be Forgotten**: Users can request permanent deletion of all account and telemetry data.

## Storage
- All personally identifiable information (PII) is encrypted at rest in the database.
- Telemetry data is anonymized for global model training unless user opts-out.
