# Export Control Guidance

The H.O.P.E. project includes cryptographic technology and performance-enhancing software that may be subject to export regulations.

### 1. EAR99 Status
Most H.O.P.E. software components are intended to be classified as EAR99 (Export Administration Regulations). However, specific high-grade encryption implementations or dual-use diagnostic patterns may require further review.

### 2. General Restrictions
You may not export, re-export, or transfer the Software to:
- Any country subject to a U.S. or international trade embargo (e.g., Cuba, Iran, North Korea, Syria).
- Any person listed on a "Denied Persons" or "Specially Designated Nationals" list.

### 3. Responsibility
It is the user's responsibility to comply with their local laws regarding the import/export of cryptographic materials and automotive engineering software.
